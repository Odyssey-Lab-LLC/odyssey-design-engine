Human Potential & AI Philosophy

Critical concern: AI, when used mindlessly, can rapidly degrade people's skills, self-efficacy, and decision-making ability. We're seeing this in some organizations and education contexts already.

Our approach: Use AI to unlock human potential, not replace human thinking. We want team members who:

  • Stay intellectually engaged (not passive consumers of AI output)
  • Verify and improve AI work (not accept blindly)
  • Grow their skills while leveraging AI (not atrophy through delegation)
  • See AI as collaborative tool (not authoritative oracle)

In practice:

  • AI assists in research, pattern recognition, creative exploration
  • Humans bring context, judgment, strategic vision, lived experience
  • Collaborative review (not AI-generated-then-accepted) produces best outcomes
  • We measure whether AI use is amplifying capability or creating dependency

Why this matters for your role: Building hiring and culture systems (recruiting → integration → growth → enablement pipeline) that preserve and enhance human capability while leveraging AI is core to the Saga vision. The systems we build should make people more capable, not less.

The Hero's Journey Ethos

We embrace the hero's journey as our operating philosophy, recognizing that roles shift based on whose story is being told:

  • For clients: They're the heroes of their story; we serve as guides and mentors on their journey
  • Within the team: We're a party of adventurers with complementary strengths - sometimes leading, sometimes supporting, always collaborating
  • As a company: We're building a legacy that outlasts any individual - systems and wisdom worth inheriting

This isn't metaphor - it's operational philosophy. Gamification (quest logs, character progression, party dynamics) isn't frivolous, it's engagement architecture that makes sustainable excellence possible.

Your role in this: You'd help build the operational systems AND the culture systems that make this philosophy real, not just aspirational.
